Palo Verde Boat Safari

A 1.5-hour boat ride down the Tempisque River through one of Central America's most important wetlands. Crocodiles basking on the banks, howler monkeys in the trees, and hundreds of bird species — this is the best wildlife experience accessible from the JW Marriott.

The experience

What to expect

Palo Verde National Park protects one of the last remaining tropical dry forests in Central America, along with vast seasonal wetlands on the Tempisque River. The boat safari takes you through the heart of this ecosystem on a covered motorboat with a naturalist guide.

April is actually an excellent time to visit — as the dry season peaks, animals concentrate around the remaining water sources, making them much easier to spot. Expect to see American crocodiles up close (some over 3 meters), white-faced capuchins, howler monkeys, green iguanas, and an astonishing variety of birds including roseate spoonbills, jabiru storks, great blue herons, tiger herons, and dozens more.

  • 1.5-hour covered motorboat ride on the Tempisque River
  • American crocodiles — often within a few meters of the boat
  • 250+ bird species recorded in the park
  • Howler monkeys and white-faced capuchins in riverside trees
  • Massive green iguanas on every branch
  • Naturalist guide identifies all wildlife in English and Spanish
  • April advantage: dry season concentrates animals near water
  • Family-owned operator with 30+ years of experience

Practical info

How to book & what to bring

  • Booking: Palo Verde Boat Tours (family-owned, 30+ years), Tour Guanacaste, or JW Marriott concierge
  • What to bring: Binoculars, camera with zoom lens, hat, sunscreen, water, insect repellent
  • What's included: Boat ride, naturalist guide, park entrance fee (check with operator)
  • Getting there: 1.5-hour drive from the JW Marriott. Some operators include transport.
  • Tip: Morning tours see the most active wildlife. Bring binoculars — the birdwatching is world-class.
  • Contact: Palo Verde Boat Tours — info@paloverdeboattours.com, +506 8841 8943
