Two bases, best of both worlds
Costa Rica · Apr 16–22

The Sweet Spot

Split the trip between the Central Pacific coast (Los Sueños Marriott, Manuel Antonio, Quepos fishing) and the Arenal volcano region (hot springs, hanging bridges, sloths). One hotel change, maximum variety.

Day by day Pros & cons
Hotels

Where you’ll stay

  • Airport hotel (night 0 & 5)
  • Los Sueños Marriott Ocean & Golf Resort (nights 1–3, Marriott points)
  • La Fortuna / Arenal lodge (night 4)

Pros

  • Best wildlife variety — monkeys on beaches, sloths in cloud forest
  • Two distinct experiences: Pacific coast + volcano rainforest
  • Marriott points for Los Sueños, only one hotel change

Cons

  • Day 4 has a long drive (4 hours coast to volcano)
  • Less time at each location than a single-base trip
  • Miss the Guanacaste Gold Coast beaches

Day by day

The Sweet Spot

Full breakdown from arrival to departure.

Day 0 · Thu Apr 16

Arrive & Settle

Land at SJO at 10:35 PM. Quick transfer to airport hotel.

  • Air Transat TS 112 lands at 10:35 PM
  • Shuttle to airport hotel
  • Quick dinner and early night
  • Tomorrow: 2–3 hour drive to the Central Pacific coast
Day 1 · Fri Apr 17

Central Pacific Coast

2–3 hour drive to Los Sueños Marriott. Afternoon beach time on the Central Pacific coast, sunset over Herradura Bay.

  • Pick up rental car, drive to Los Sueños / Herradura (2–3 hours)
  • Check in at Los Sueños Marriott Ocean & Golf Resort
  • Afternoon at the resort beach and pools
  • Optional: drive 1 hour south to Manuel Antonio for a preview
  • Sunset over Herradura Bay
  • Dinner at the resort or in Jacó town
Day 2 · Sat Apr 18

Manuel Antonio National Park

Full day at Manuel Antonio: guided tour through the park spotting capuchins, howler monkeys, sloths, and iguanas. Swim at pristine park beaches.

  • Drive to Manuel Antonio (1 hour south)
  • Guided tour of the national park (book ahead — limited daily entry)
  • Spot white-faced capuchins, howler monkeys, two-toed sloths
  • Swim at Playa Manuel Antonio — monkeys on the beach
  • Lunch at a park-adjacent restaurant
  • Return to Los Sueños for evening at the resort
Day 3 · Sun Apr 19

Fishing & Remote Beaches

Morning fishing charter from Quepos (the sailfish capital), then afternoon exploring remote beaches near Dominical.

  • Early morning drive to Quepos marina (1 hour)
  • Half-day fishing charter — sailfish, mahi-mahi, dorado
  • Drive south to Dominical for a wild, uncrowded beach afternoon
  • Spot howler monkeys in the trees along the coast road
  • Fresh tropical fruit from roadside stands
  • Return to Los Sueños for final night at the Marriott
Day 4 · Mon Apr 20

Arenal Volcano & Hot Springs

4-hour drive from the coast to La Fortuna. Arenal volcano views, afternoon hanging bridges walk, and evening volcanic hot springs.

  • Morning checkout from Los Sueños, 4-hour drive to La Fortuna
  • Check in to Arenal lodge with volcano views
  • Afternoon at Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges — canopy walk through the rainforest
  • Spot sloths, toucans, and howler monkeys from the bridges
  • Evening soak in Tabacón or Baldi volcanic hot springs
  • Dinner with a view of Arenal Volcano
Day 5 · Tue Apr 21

Sloths, Waterfalls & Return

Morning sloth spotting and La Fortuna waterfall, then 2.5-hour drive back to SJO. Overnight near the airport.

  • Early guided sloth and wildlife walk near Arenal
  • Visit La Fortuna Waterfall (if time allows)
  • Late morning drive back to San José area (2.5 hours)
  • Stop for Costa Rican coffee and souvenirs
  • Check in to airport hotel
  • Early night — Emilie & Madison fly at 7:55 AM
