Day by day

The Explorer

Full breakdown from arrival to departure.

Day 0 · Thu Apr 16

Arrive & Settle

Land at SJO at 10:35 PM. Shuttle to airport hotel for the night.

  • Air Transat TS 112 lands at 10:35 PM
  • Shuttle to airport hotel
  • Quick dinner and early night
  • Tomorrow: 3-hour drive to Manuel Antonio
Day 1 · Fri Apr 17

Manuel Antonio Arrival

3-hour drive south to Manuel Antonio. Afternoon on the beach with capuchin monkeys in the trees overhead.

  • Pick up rental car and head south on Costanera highway
  • 3-hour drive to Manuel Antonio (scenic Pacific coast road)
  • Check in to boutique hotel near the park
  • Afternoon at Playa Espadilla — capuchin monkeys roam the beach
  • Sunset from the Manuel Antonio hillside
  • Dinner in Quepos town
Day 2 · Sat Apr 18

Park & Fishing

Morning guided tour of Manuel Antonio National Park (monkeys, sloths, beaches), then afternoon fishing charter from Quepos.

  • Early entry to Manuel Antonio National Park with a guide
  • Spot white-faced capuchins, howler monkeys, and two- and three-toed sloths
  • Swim at the park’s hidden beaches
  • Afternoon fishing charter out of Quepos marina
  • Chase sailfish, mahi-mahi, and roosterfish
  • Dinner with fresh catch in Quepos
Day 3 · Sun Apr 19

Arenal & Hot Springs

4-hour drive inland to La Fortuna. Afternoon sloth spotting, evening soak in volcanic hot springs with Arenal views.

  • Morning checkout, 4-hour drive to La Fortuna / Arenal
  • Stop for roadside tropical fruit along the way
  • Check in to Arenal lodge with volcano views
  • Afternoon sloth-spotting walk (guided, near Arenal)
  • Evening at Tabacón or Baldi hot springs
  • Dinner with Arenal Volcano glowing in the background
Day 4 · Mon Apr 20

Bridges, Waterfalls & Fruit

Arenal hanging bridges and La Fortuna waterfall in the morning, then drive to Sarapiquí for chocolate and tropical fruit farms.

  • Mistico Arenal Hanging Bridges — walk through the canopy
  • La Fortuna Waterfall hike (500 steps down and back up)
  • Drive to Sarapiquí (1.5 hours through lush lowlands)
  • Chocolate and tropical fruit farm tour
  • Taste exotic fruits: rambutan, starfruit, guanábana
  • Overnight at Sarapiquí eco-lodge
Day 5 · Tue Apr 21

Fruit Market & Return

Morning at a local fruit market, then 2-hour drive back to SJO. Last-minute shopping, overnight near the airport.

  • Morning visit to local fruit and produce market
  • Stock up on Costa Rican coffee and chocolate
  • 2-hour drive back to San José
  • Souvenir shopping in Escazú or Multiplaza
  • Check in to airport hotel
  • Early night — Emilie & Madison fly at 7:55 AM
