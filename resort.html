The Resort

Full breakdown from arrival to departure.

Day 0 · Thu Apr 16

Arrive & Settle

Land at SJO at 10:35 PM. Short shuttle to airport hotel, crash for the night.

  • Air Transat TS 112 lands at 10:35 PM
  • Shuttle to airport hotel (Marriott San José or Holiday Inn Express)
  • Quick dinner at hotel or nearby
  • Early night — big drive tomorrow
Day 1 · Fri Apr 17

Drive to Guanacaste

4-hour scenic drive northwest to the JW Marriott Guanacaste. Settle in and catch sunset on the beach.

  • Pick up rental car at SJO
  • 4-hour scenic drive along the Pan-American Highway
  • Check in at JW Marriott Guanacaste Resort & Spa
  • Explore the resort — pools, beach, grounds
  • Golden-hour walk on Mansita Beach
  • Sunset dinner at the resort
Day 2 · Sat Apr 18

Deep-Sea Fishing

Morning fishing charter out of Flamingo or Tamarindo. Chase sailfish and mahi-mahi in the Pacific, then relax poolside.

  • Early departure for Flamingo marina (30 min drive)
  • Half-day deep-sea fishing charter — sailfish, mahi-mahi, roosterfish
  • Return to resort by early afternoon
  • Pool and beach afternoon
  • Fresh catch dinner if the crew cleans your fish
Day 3 · Sun Apr 19

Remote Beach Hopping

Discover hidden beaches along the Gold Coast: Playa Conchal’s crushed-shell shore, Playa San Juanillo, and howler monkeys in the trees.

  • Drive to Playa Conchal — crushed seashell beach, clear snorkeling
  • Continue to Playa San Juanillo — secluded and quiet
  • Spot howler monkeys in the coastal forest canopy
  • Tropical fruit from roadside stands — mango, cas, guanábana
  • Sunset back at the resort
Day 4 · Mon Apr 20

Tamarindo & Shopping

Beach day in Tamarindo with surfing, boutique bathing-suit shopping, and tropical fruit stands.

  • Drive to Tamarindo (30 min)
  • Morning surf lesson or beach time
  • Boutique shopping — bathing suits, local crafts
  • Tropical fruit tasting — fresh juices and smoothies
  • Lunch at a beachfront restaurant
  • Return to resort for final sunset swim
Day 5 · Tue Apr 21

Last Beach & Return

Morning beach walk, then 4-hour drive back to the SJO area. Overnight near the airport for early flights.

  • Final sunrise beach walk
  • Late morning checkout from JW Marriott
  • 4-hour drive back to San José area
  • Stop for souvenirs and coffee along the way
  • Check in to airport hotel
  • Early night — Emilie & Madison fly out at 7:55 AM
